CampusCraze

CampusCraze is a mobile application built to solve a real problem I personally faced.

The Problem:

Students often receive symposium and college event posters after the registration deadline or even after the event date.

The Solution:

CampusCraze provides a platform where educational event posters can be shared in a timely manner, helping students stay updated and avoid missing opportunities.

Tech Used:

Flutter (Dart), Firebase Firestore, Firebase Authentication, SQLite
